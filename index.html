About Us

Introduction

We are one of Nepal’s largest manufacturers and distributors of confectionery and gum. We create imaginative products that are enjoyed by the consumers.

Vision

We will enhance our world leadership in confectionery by creating value for consumers through innovative and gratifying high-quality products.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with guaranteed best quality confectionary products maintaining excellent services, easy availability, regularity in supply and quality consistency.

Why are we the best?





Quality

Consumers around the world want to be sure that the products they buy are of unparalleled quality. We pay the utmost attention to selecting the finest raw materials, developing cutting-edge production processes and investing in research into newer and better production formulae.

Innovation

Our consumers in both mature and growing economies are very sensitive to new experiences and concepts. We want to be the ones who pleasantly surprise them with new products, new flavours, new packaging and new product concepts that will help them enjoy “sweeter” moments in their lives. This is why we give paramount strategic importance to researching and developing new products.

Marketing

We place great importance on understanding the market and its trends in order to identify and anticipate consumer demands. We also want to remain in tune with consumers after they have bought our products. To do this, we regularly develop effective promotional tools, high-impact and eye-catching advertising campaigns and innovative digital experiences connected to our brands.

Enviroment

Respect for the environment, not only by complying with local laws and regulations but also through trying to minimise the impact of our operations, is a vital principle at Sweet Mart. Improving the efficiency of our production processes to reduce waste, making the best use of natural resources and looking for environmentally-friendly options in our packaging choices and distribution strategies are key to executing this ideal.

People

Behind our success lies the initiative, enthusiasm, passion, dedication and creativity of our people. We are committed to creating a stimulating work environment, guaranteeing safe working conditions in our factories and offering Group-wide development and career opportunities.

Fun Facts

If you are chewing on gum while cutting onions, this will stop your eyes from watering. The science behind it? While chewing on gum you will breathe through your mouth rather than your nose so the irritation caused by the onion scent will reach the lachrymal gland in smaller amounts.

Another fun fact about chewing gum is that it has two national days on September 30th and on February 15th. Celebrate by blowing some bubbles!

Have you ever wondered why most chewing gum is pink? Well, an interesting fun fact about chewing gum is that the inventor was desperately trying to create the perfect bubble gum, and by the time the recipe reached perfection, the only colour he had left to use was pink!
The history of chewing gum makes for a fascinating read.

We all grew up being told that if you swallow chewing gum, your intestines will get clogged up and your intestines will get stuck together. Sound familiar? Well in actual fact, this is actually quite rare.
If you swallow chewing gum, it will be in your system for a few days, before your body will get rid of it. As gum base can’t be digested, you will eliminate it as a whole. If you want to read more about what happens when you swallow chewing gum, read our blog post!

An interesting of chewing gum fact is that it can help you concentrate. Studies have shown that when you chew on gum you become more alert, your muscle tension is reduced and you are able to focus better on the activities you are working on.

If you’ve ever been on a plane, you will know how it feels for your ears to pop. But you don’t need to worry anymore! A useful fact is that chewing gum can prevent your ears from popping when you’re changing altitude levels on a plane. This is because you produce more saliva when you chew on gum and swallowing helps balance the pressure in your head. So grab some ChewsyGum before heading abroad!

The only animals that can chew on gum are humans! Interestingly, despite studies suggesting we originate from the monkey family, when monkeys were given chewing gum for the first time, they chewed on it for a few minutes before taking it out and sticking it to their fur.

Another useful fun fact about chewing gum is that you can remove it from your hair using peanut butter. You will have to separate the area from the rest of your hair, cover it in peanut butter and start rubbing it out. This is due to the oil found in peanut butter which helps to separate the gum. Hopefully the monkeys knew this trick too!
There are other ways in which you can remove chewing gum from your hair, but let’s face it, this is the tastiest way to do it.

Do you often get heartburn after eating? The best way to combat this is to chew on some gum! Studies have shown that having a chewing gum after a meal helps with heartburn as it can reduce acid in the esophagus.

The first name for chewing gum was “Blibber-Blubber”. Can you imagine if that stuck? It’s a bit of a mouthful to say the least!
